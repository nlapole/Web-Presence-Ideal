Vermont Studio Center Residency 2018

Johnson, Vermont, 2018

This residency was by far one of the more impactful experiences I've had in the arts. Excellent people who, in the context of a 'summer camp for artists', really shone for how generous they were as people. From karaoke to star-gazing, from games of double-speed to artists talks... even the spiders beside my bed and the drunken stupor served a purpose here. Structures, translations, game mechanics, and probed anxieties were besides the point among fellow creatives turned friends.
